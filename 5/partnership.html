Partnership

The partnership program is designed for those who have completed our ceremonies or mentorship.

Partnership Model
You recommend — we lead the process. For each person who joins through your recommendation, you receive 20%. The distribution model is 80/20.

Financial Model
Standard format — 80/20. Total group revenue – retreat costs = net profit. From the net profit: 80% remains with the organizers, 20% goes to the partner.

Extended format — 70/30
If third parties are involved, we offer: 70% — organizers, 30% — partner.

Special Conditions
Partners may attend the retreat together with the group, paying only the base cost (medicine, accommodation, meals, organizational expenses) — no markup.
